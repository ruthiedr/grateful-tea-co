welcome, tea lover.

hello!

we are a family-owned business that has transformed a passion for rooibos tea into a sustainable tea company. We take pride in our efforts to reduce our environmental impact, our certification as a fair-trade company, and the unique rooibos tea blends we've created and come to love.

about us

our tea is organically grown in South Africa by equitably employed farmers who have been growing rooibos for generations.

our tea bags are plastic-free and fully compostable. we always try to make environmentally responsible choices with the production materials used.

our tea flavors are well-tested in terms of taste and experience, and the additional ingredients found in our tea blends are all locally sourced.
